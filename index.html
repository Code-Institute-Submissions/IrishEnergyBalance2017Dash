About these graphs

Source of Data

The data source for these graphs is a report called Energy in Ireland 2018 which was prepared by SEAI, and published in December 2018.

Technology Used

The data was downloaded as .xlsx, converted to .json and furtherly reorganised using a Python script.

The interactive graphs were made using a javascript charting library called dc.js, which was built to work natively with crossfilter and leverages d3.js library.

Final Energy Consumption

  • 57% of Irelands final energy consumption coming from Oil.
  • Transport energy demand is nearly twice that of the next largest consumer residential.
  • 18% of the nations energy is consumed by private vehicles
  • Industry and transport are the two biggest consumers of renewable energy (excluding electricity generated by renewble energy)
